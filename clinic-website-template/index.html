Product offerings

Ways in which Ona AI is involved:

For the blind & visually impaired

Ona AI uses speech guidance to transform digital content into an accessible experience for the blind and visually impaired, turning every interaction into a seamless journey.

For the physically disabled

Ona AI empowers individuals with physical disabilities by enabling hands-free, intuitive control of digital environments to perform complex tasks.

For the neurodivergent

Ona AI adapts digital experiences to suit the unique needs of neurodivergent individuals, creating a more focused and personalized interaction.

For the elderly & those under care

Ona AI enhances digital accessibility for the elderly and those under care, providing an intuitive and supportive experience tailored to their needs.
